About Our School

The First CBSE School in Attappady

St Gregorios English Medium School is the first CBSE English Medium School in Attappady, located in a tranquil area blessed with scenic beauty at Nellipathy, Agali — just 2 km from Goolikkadavu town towards Chittoor dam.

Named after St Gregorios of Parumala, our school was founded on the belief that education should impart not only knowledge but also culture, religious values, and character. At SGEMS, the management and teaching faculty uphold this vision of holistic education.
